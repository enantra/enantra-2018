Strategy Master

DESCRIPTION :

Its time to unleash the master inside you! Strategy master is an event drafted to focus on the financial areas of business. It is formed to analyse and develop the distinctive skills of an entrepreneur to handle and tackle money in terms of an intricate financial crisis.

FORMAT   :

The event will contain two rounds each round will test the skills of the students in tackling real life financial crisis. Realistic approach is appreciated. Each team will be judged based on various criteria and the best will be awarded.

RULES :

  • It is a group event.
  • Teams will be judged based on relevance, creativity, probability of occurrence, time management and budget allocation.
  • Judges decision will be final.

E-Idol

DESCRIPTION :

If you have what it takes to rule the business world join us on an ode to become an idol. E-idol is an event which is one of a kind , it tests the ability and measures the capacity of an individual to become a sole proprietor.

FORMAT   :

The event consists of 4 rounds each of which will test every aspect of the individual with respect to spontaneity quick thinking.

  • Round 1 : Firstly, the participants  have to introduce themselves and describe their likes and dislikes in the best possible way.
  • Round 2 : The second round is block and tackle which will test one’s spontaneity and quick thinking.
  • Round 3 : The participant has to create and describe the details of a company individually all by themselves. The best idea will be selected for the final round.
  • Round 4 : The final round is a SURPRISE ROUND.

RULES :

  • The event involves individual participation.
  • Participants will be judged on various criteria such as relevance, creativity and presentation.
  • Judges decision will be final.

Spin The Wheel

DESCRIPTION :

Want to have some fun? This event is best suited for you. The game has a spinning wheel. Every sector on the wheel has a prize and a task stuck to it. The participant has to spin it and complete the relevant task to win the prize. If you get lucky you get the chance to win amazing prizes!

FORMAT   :

The spinning wheel can be spun only once. The task associated can be an enactment of a situation such as product marketing, If a re-spin occurs, the participant gets to spin the wheel one more time.

RULES :

  • Individual / team participation.
  • Participant performance needs to be relevant to the task.
  • They will be judged on the basis of creative  thinking, time management, relevance to the given task.
  • Prize will not be given if the performance is not relevant.

Quiz

DESCRIPTION :

Are a business geek? Someone who is aware about all the aspects of entrepreneurship? Put your knowledge into action by taking a simple quiz and walk away with exciting prizes.

FORMAT   :

The event consists of 2 rounds.

  • Round 1 :  The 1st  round will be a paper-pen quiz. Participants will be given a questionnaire which needs to be solved in 30  mins. Selected participants will be qualified to the next round.
  • Round 2  :  The 2nd  level is a buzzer round.

RULES :

  • Group/ Individual  participation.
  • Phones are not allowed.
  • Usage of internet is restricted.
  • No negative marking.
  • Min of 3 teams will be qualified for finals.
  • Teams that are suspected for malpractices will be disqualified on spot.

GRANDMASTER..!!

DESCRIPTION :

Who knew that a phrase just as simple as a yes or a no will make you win an entire event. Well this is GRANSMASTER, an interesting event that puts your knowledge to test. Questions will be thrown by the participant for which the answer will be mere yes or no. So who guesses the personality in the grandmasters mind will be named master himself!

FORMAT   :

One of the  Enantra  member will be the  grandmaster. Every participant gets 50 mins time. The participant has to find the personality the grandmaster is thinking of within 21 questions. If the game exceeds the time limit, then the participant is disqualified.

The personality to be found changes regardless of each game’s status.

RULES :

  • This event is based on individual participation.
  • Personalities will be strictly entrepreneurs.
  • Only 21 questions are allowed.
  • Grandmaster will give either a yes or no as an answer.
  • Violation of rules leads to disqualification.
  • Phones/ internet usage is strictly prohibited during the game
  • Judges decision will be final.

MARKETING MARATHON..!!

DESCRIPTION :

Do you have the ability to sell anything and everything? Can you woo your buyers with your exceptional persuading skills? An event entirely dedicated to all the spontaneous people out there, in which participants get to advertise a product or a service.

FORMAT   :

The event consists of 4 rounds.

  • ROUND 1 : The first round is similar to Adzap. An innovative Product will be provided to each team and they will be given 10 mins to discuss. The team has to present their idea.
  • ROUND 2 : In the 2nd round, each team  has to prepare and enact a 1-min ad for the same product that was given in the first round. Best team will be selected for the 3rd round.
  • ROUND 3 : The 3rd round is an on-going promotion round where the teams have to promote their product to a large crowd.
  • ROUND 4 : In the final round, the teams have to campaign for a cause (for the society, environment, etc…)

NOTES :

Marketing marathon has a pre-requisite round which gives direct entry to the 2nd  round. The team has to register earlier and market an Enantra poster. The poster with max likes gets selected for the 2nd  round directly.

RULES :

  • The event includes a Group\ Individual participation.
  • udged based on creativity, innovative thinking, performance, presentation, relevance to the given product.
  • Judges decision will be final.

UBER PITCH..!!

DESCRIPTION :

Who knew a cab ride could fetch you a chance to make your start-up a reality. Uber pitch is an event for aspiring entrepreneurs and  self-starters. Your big business break could be just an Uber away! Uber pitch basically consists of 2 rounds. Uber Pitch will be held as a two day event, with rounds being held on consecutive days.

FORMAT   :

  • ROUND 1 :  A filer round which takes 10 best ideas into the second. Group of  maximum of 4 will be formed and will pitch their  business model to the judges. The maximum time given to a team to pitch is 10 minutes. Judges will be select 10 teams who go on to the next round, which will be held on day 2.
  • ROUND 2 : Each contestant/team will  take an Uber ride (one at a time) around the campus and will be asked to pitch their ideas to the judge accompanying them. The judge will be representative of Uber India or an investor, and has about 7-10 minutes to pitch. The best team walks away with the cash prize. In the rare occurrence of a tie, the prize money will be split amongst the 2 winners.

RULES :

  • Group/Individual participation.
  •  Judged based on creativity, innovative thinking, performance, and new and fresh ideas.
  • Judges decision will be final.

PUZZLED

DESCRIPTION :

For all the puzzle geeks out there, this is a wonderful chance to win yourself some exciting prizes. Puzzled is an event that will put your knowledge and patience to test as time runs fast.

FORMAT   :

The event consists of 2 rounds.  

  • ROUND 1 :  Teams of 6 is formed. Each team is given a  distantly different jigsaw puzzle of equal difficulty. 20 minutes will be given to complete the puzzle as a group. Some of the pieces in their puzzle belongs to the other puzzles in the room.
  • ROUND 2 : The participants are divided into two groups. One group will be buyers and the other group will be sellers. Buyers and sellers then interact in a market place by buying and selling a virtual product. The prices for the virtual product are guided by buyer cards and seller cards, each containing a written price. The price indicated on the card is the price at which the virtual product must be sold or bought to break even.

RULES :

  • This can be a Group/ Individual participation
  • Each person should assume a role like barter,  merger, etc..
  • They should present their points for winning to the judges.
  • Teams will be judged based on creativity, innovation, thinking, performance, presentation, relevance to the given product.
  • Judges decision will be final.

BIZ WIZARD

DESCRIPTION :

Abracadabra! Your chance to use a magic spell to win some exciting cash prizes is here! Biz Wizard has 3 rounds. In each round, the participants should use internet to find the answer. Every participant must have some internet accessible devices like mobile, laptop, tablet. Each team consists of two members.

FORMAT :

  • ROUND 1 :  Logical questions that based on successful entrepreneurs will be asked. Some technical clues will be provided for certain questions.  Registration is limited to 25 teams.
  • ROUND 2 : The participants should submit the business plan with some statistics like how much they can earn within a year. Based on the different good ideas 5 teams will be selected to final round.
  • ROUND 3 : This round contains cryptography, interesting short story based questions. The clues are present inside the story . If you extract the clues you can find the answer. Some surprise questions are included in this level.

RULES :

  • Each team consists of two members.
  • Each team must have some internet accessible devices.
  • Teams will be judged based on time management, creativity, innovative thinking.
  • Judges decision will be final.

HEAR MY VOICE

DESCRIPTION :

An event of one of its kind, the title explains its uniqueness! HEAR MY VOICE is a group event which mainly tests the communication skills of an individual.

FORMAT   :

  • ROUND 1 :   PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT

    • Each participant will be given a personality, they have to assume themselves as the personality given and give a 2-3 minutes talk about themselves and their life story. Participants can use the internet to research about the personality before submitting their speech.

  • ROUND 2 : FISHBOWL CONVERSATION

    • In a fish bowl conversation panel, 2 to 3 chairs are filled with guest panellist & 1 chair is left empty for audience members .The moderator introduces the topic & panellist start discussing it.    Any member of audience can occupy the empty chair at any time & start the conversation at the same time an existing member must voluntarily leave the fishbowl & free a chair.  The discussion continues with participants frequently entering & leaving the panel. Moderators summarize the discussion.

    RULES :

    • Each team consists of two members.
    • Each team must have some internet accessible devices.
    • Judged based on time management, creativity, innovative thinking.
    • Judges decision will be final.

    StartUp Pitch Fest

    DESCRIPTION :

    Start-Up Pitchfest will provide an opportunity to several start-up enthusiasts to showcase their ideas and acquire the needed support to scale-up their innovation. The event is organized by Capitalize- The student Entrepreneurship Club of CEG Anna University and powered by the Entrepreneurship Development Institute of India-Tamil Nadu (EDII-TN) a well-known government organization aiming to inculcate and encourage new start-up ventures. Start-Up Pitchfest is a state-wide event with registrations open to candidates from all around Chennai. The teams will have to pitch their ideas to an expert jury panel consisting of venture capitalists and investors. The team with the best pitch will be given the required pre-seed fund.

    Entre-tainment is a high profile dinner setup for the sole purpose of providing a complacent atmosphere wherein interaction with top business personnel is provided. Innovative titles will be awarded to various executives based on market surveys.
